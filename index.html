Our Products

Cartons

Our sturdy and customizable cartons are designed for safe packaging and transportation of garments. They are available in various sizes and can be tailored to meet your branding and logistic needs.

Gum Tapes

Our high-adhesion gum tapes provide secure sealing for packaging, ensuring garments remain intact during transit. These tapes are strong, durable, and easy to use.

Poly Sheets

We supply premium-quality poly sheets, ideal for protecting garments from dust, moisture, and damage. These sheets are lightweight, transparent, and recyclable, ensuring safe storage and handling.

Sewing Threads

Our high-strength sewing threads are manufactured to ensure smooth and reliable stitching for all types of garments. They are available in a wide range of colors and specifications to suit every requirement.

Elastic Bands

Designed for comfort and elasticity, our elastic bands are suitable for a variety of garment applications. They are available in multiple sizes, colors, and textures, offering excellent stretch and recovery.
